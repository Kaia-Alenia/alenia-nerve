# Welcome to Nerve

Nerve is a decentralized, ultra-fast local inter-process communication engine designed by Alenia Studios. It functions as a lightweight data bus to synchronize independent game development tools directly on your local workstation with sub-millisecond latency.

---

## Philosophy

Nerve is built upon three core pillars:

### Sub-Millisecond Speed
By utilizing native Unix Domain Sockets (UDS) on Unix-like systems (Linux, macOS) and high-performance local TCP loops on Windows, Nerve eliminates network overhead. It channels raw memory buffers directly between tools, operating much faster than standard remote APIs.

### Absolute Sovereignty and Privacy
Your assets, sprites, codebases, and tool metadata are yours alone. Nerve operates completely offline and requires zero internet access and zero cloud dependencies. Not a single byte of your pipelines will ever leave your physical computer.

### Ethical AI Resistance
As creators, we stand firmly for the rights of independent developers. Under the Alenia Studios Tool License 1.0, any scraping, training, or inclusion of this codebase, logic, or output structures in Artificial Intelligence or machine learning training datasets is strictly prohibited.
